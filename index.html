About me
In a nutshell: I’m a tech‑oriented creative.
I’m passionate about technologies and UX design lets me tie them to my goal: making people’s lives easier while being engaging through sharp storytelling. As in any design process, technical limits are part of the journey; it’s my call to find feasible solutions. Obstacles can become opportunities, too. I believe in cooperation, working with everyone involved in a project: developers, product owners, analysts, and stakeholders to meet shared needs.
